Instructions

Fight your way through a casino and gather dice along the way.

By collecting dice you will gain increased stats at the end of every round.

Your goal is to reach as far as possible without dying.



Your health is indicated by the number on top of your head.

At the end of every round you will skip an amount of stages based on your performance in the round.



Move around with WASD and shoot with LMB.
